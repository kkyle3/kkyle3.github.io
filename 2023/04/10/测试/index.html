Baskteball shooting analysis

This video explains how to use full body strength to form a good shooting motion. You need to stand with your feet parallel and apart to stabilize your body balance during shooting, then make your elbows bend at a 90 degree angle, lift the ball to your shoulder position, and squat down to make a shot.
